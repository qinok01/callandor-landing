Who are we?

We are Callandor, a tight-knit crew of hackers, makers, and innovators from Brown University.

Born in the labs and dorms of College Hill in May 2024, we're on a mission to push the boundaries of what's possible as builders.

Our team is a melting pot of coding wizardry - from the artisanal UI/UX crafter to the deep learning architect, the full-stack ninja to the blockchain mastermind. We live and breathe the hacker ethos of rapid prototyping, failing fast, and pivoting to perfection.

After training and building throughout summer 2024, we're hitting the road and taking our talents to the biggest hackathons across the country. From the sunny shores of Cali to the concrete jungle of NYC, we'll be burning the midnight oil, downing energy drinks, and dropping jaws with our slick, disruptive tech.

But we're not just in it for the glory and the swag. At our core, Callandor is about leveraging our powers for good. We're laser-focused on hacking solutions to the most pressing problems and spinning out projects into more long term builds.

So whether you're a fellow hacker, a curious onlooker, or a recruiter scouting the next big thing, keep an eye on Callandor. We're coming to a hackathon near you, ready to break things, and change the world.
